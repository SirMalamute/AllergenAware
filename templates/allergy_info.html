Tips to Prevent Cross-Contamination & Be Allergen-Friendly

Here are some essential tips to help you prevent cross-contamination and maintain a safe environment for those with allergies:

  • Always use separate utensils and cutting boards for allergen-containing and allergen-free foods.
  • Thoroughly wash your hands, surfaces, and tools after handling any allergen-containing food.
  • Label foods clearly if they contain allergens to avoid accidental exposure.
  • Store allergen-free foods separately from allergen-containing foods to prevent cross-contact.
  • Be mindful when preparing food in shared kitchens, especially if allergen-friendly alternatives are involved.
  • Read food labels carefully to ensure products are free of allergens or produced in allergen-free facilities.
